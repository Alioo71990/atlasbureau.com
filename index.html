About Atlas Bureau: Our Projects

About Atlas Bureau:

Atlas Engineering Bureau established in 1988 and is registered in the Iraqi Engineers Union No. 104 provides services to the private sector and government organizations in the following areas.

  • Audit for granting a certificate of conformity with the requirements of ISO 9001: 2008 in cooperation with Vincotte International Middle East (VIEM) ,United Arab Emirates.
  • Auditor training under international Registrar of Certified auditors (IRCA) as a representative of the company Abacus Quality Training Services (AQTS), USA to get Certified Lead auditor certificate or internal auditor Certificate.
  • Pre certification audit of the quality management system before calling the ISO 9001 certification body (CB) including the documentation and checking the internal audit and management review results.
  • Training in the field of the environment management system in accordance with ISO 14001 requirements and in the field of occupational health and safety management system under OHSAS 18001 requirements
  • Practical application of statistical methods in quality (SQC) and the seven tools of quality used in Japan.

Our Goals

The Bureau aims at introducing the latest methods of quality control, quality management and auditing of its systems to the companies, and the productive, service and educational organizations that are contracted to achieve:

First Goal

• Taking the quality to high place of the products and services.

Second Goal

• Reduce the cost of quality by controlling and preventing quality deviations before they occur.

Third Goal

• Training the staff in the field of quality control and the transfer of theoretical and practical experience to enable them to perform their work with the required quality.

Fourth Goal

• Creating a work environment that promotes synergy and creativity
